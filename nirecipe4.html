Fresh Fish Pepper Soup

Ingredients

  • Pepper soup spices
  • Ozazi leaves
  • Scent leaves
  • Maggi cubes
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Lemon
  • Onious
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Crayfish
  • Half stalk of lemon grass to eliminate the taste of raw fish (optional)
  • 5 balls Pepper(Atarodo) (Desired quantity)
  • Cameron pepper (optional)
Preparation
  • 1. Wash fish with salt and lemon.

  • 2. Boil fish with seasoning cubes, salt, pepper, onious, ginger.

  • 3. Bring to boiling point then add pepper soup spices.

  • 4. Then add scent leaf or oziza leaf.

  • 5. Add crayfish.

  • 6. Allow to simmer for 3 minutes and pepper soup is ready.
