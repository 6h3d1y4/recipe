Efo Riro (Vegetable Soup)

Ingredients

  • Soko leaves also known as Lagos Spinach or argentea leaf, African spinach
  • Dry Fish
  • Meat
  • Shaki, kpomo, (assorted meat)
  • Cow leg
  • Goat meat
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Locust bean
  • Bell pepper (tatahse)
  • Atarodo (fresh peper)
  • Palm oil
  • Crayfish
Preparation
  • 1. Marinate all proteins with seasoning cubes, salt, dry ginger/garlic powder, onious and grinder pepper and bring to boiling point.

  • 2. Fry onions in palm oil and add blended tatahse (long red ball pepper), locust beans, dry pepper and grinded fresh pepper. Fry until itâ€™s dry.

  • 3. Pour hot boiling water into the soko leaves and soak it for a minute and drain. This is to make the leaves more absorbents in the sauce.
  • 4. Add meat stock into already fried pepper sauce and stir and bring to boiling point.

  • 5. Leave to simmer for 2 minutes and your Efo Riro is ready.

  • 6. When the meat is done, turn off the heat and serve immediately.
